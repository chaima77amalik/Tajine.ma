Special Menu

We Make The Best Morocco Food In
Our Restaurant

Tagine

There are many types of tagine, such as lamb with prunes, chicken with olives, and fish tagine. It is slow-cooked over low heat to keep the meat tender and the flavors rich.

Couscous

The national dish of Morocco, usually prepared on Fridays with vegetables, chickpeas, and meat (lamb or chicken), served with broth.

Pastilla

A luxurious dish made with crispy pastry, traditionally stuffed with chicken and almonds or seafood and shrimp. It is often served on special occasions.

Harira

A traditional Moroccan soup, commonly eaten during Ramadan. It contains tomatoes, lentils, chickpeas, and vermicelli, with a rich blend of spices.
